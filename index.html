ABOUT US

At Starbucks, we are more than just a coffee company. We are a global community that celebrates the love of coffee, connection, and a commitment to making a positive impact on the world.
Our journey began in 1971 with a single store in Seattle, and since then, we have grown into a beloved brand with thousands of locations around the globe.
But our mission remains the same - to inspire and nurture the human spirit one cup, one person, and one neighborhood at a time.
